Account & Data Deletion

How to Delete Your BatchBoss Account

You can request deletion of your BatchBoss account and all associated data at any time. Follow the steps below:

  1. Open the BatchBoss app on your device
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete
  3. Go to Account Settings from the navigation menu
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly at hello@batchboss.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your account.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your full name and email address
  • Your account password
  • Your business settings and preferences
  • All batches, items, orders, and invoices you created
  • All customer records you entered
  • Analytics data tied to your account

Retention Period

Your account and data are deleted right away upon request. We do not retain personal data after an account is deleted.
