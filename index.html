Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Introduction

BatchBoss ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the BatchBoss mobile application.

By using BatchBoss, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted, never stored in plain text)

Business Information

  • Business name
  • Business settings and preferences

App Data

  • Batch and shipment information you create
  • Customer orders you record
  • Stock and inventory data you enter
  • Invoice records
  • Analytics data based on your business activity

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your BatchBoss account
  • Store and sync your business data across sessions
  • Generate invoices and business reports
  • Provide customer support when you contact us
  • Improve the BatchBoss app and user experience
  • Send important account and service updates

4. How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is:

  • Encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Protected with Row Level Security — meaning you can only access your own data
  • Never shared with other BatchBoss users

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your personal data to anyone. We share data only with the following trusted service providers:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Resend — email delivery for invoices and account notifications

6. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be deleted right away. Your business data will also be permanently deleted when your account is closed.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your business data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: hello@batchboss.app

8. Children's Privacy

BatchBoss is designed for business owners and is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify users through the app where appropriate.
